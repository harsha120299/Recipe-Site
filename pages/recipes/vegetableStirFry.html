Vegetable Stir Fry

20 mins Easy 2 servings

A quick and healthy vegetable stir fry with tofu that's packed with flavor and ready in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 200g firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  2. Add tofu and cook until golden on all sides, about 5 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add remaining oil to the pan. Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add all vegetables and stir fry for 4-5 minutes until crisp-tender.
  5. Return tofu to the pan. Add soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil. Toss to combine.
  6. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until everything is well coated and heated through.
  7. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve immediately over rice or noodles.

Notes

For extra protein, you can substitute tofu with chicken, beef, or shrimp.

Add chili flakes or sriracha for a spicy version.
