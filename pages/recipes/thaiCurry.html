Thai Curry

35 mins Easy 4 servings

A flavorful Thai-style curry made with coconut milk, curry paste, and a mix of veggies and protein of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp red or green curry paste
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, etc.)
  • 1 cup protein (chicken, tofu, or shrimp)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooked jasmine rice, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add curry paste and fry for 1â€“2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Pour in coconut milk and stir to combine. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Add protein and cook until almost done.
  4. Add vegetables and simmer until tender and protein is fully cooked.
  5. Season with salt as needed.
  6. Serve hot with jasmine rice.

Notes

Use Thai basil or a squeeze of lime at the end for extra freshness.

Customize the spice level by adjusting the amount of curry paste.
