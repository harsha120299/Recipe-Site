Red Velvet Cake

1 hour 15 mins Medium 10 servings

Ingredients

Cake:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk, room temperature
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 2 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp white vinegar

Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 16 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 6 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

Cake:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
  3. In a large bowl, beat together oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vanilla, and vinegar.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Divide batter evenly between prepared pans. Bake for 25-30 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.

Frosting:

  1. Beat cream cheese and butter together until smooth and creamy.
  2. Gradually add powdered sugar, one cup at a time, beating well after each addition.
  3. Add vanilla and salt, then beat on high speed for 3-4 minutes until fluffy.
  4. Frost cooled cake layers, stacking evenly. Decorate with cake crumbs or red velvet crumbs if desired.

Baker's Notes

Pro Tip: For best results, use gel food coloring instead of liquid for a more vibrant red color.

Make Ahead: Cake layers can be baked 1 day in advance - wrap tightly in plastic wrap and store at room temperature.

Storage: Refrigerate frosted cake for up to 5 days. Bring to room temperature before serving.
