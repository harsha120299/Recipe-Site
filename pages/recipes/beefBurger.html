Classic Beef Burger

25 mins Easy 4 burgers

Juicy, flavorful beef burgers with all the classic toppings - perfect for your next cookout.

Ingredients

Burgers:

  • 1 1/2 lbs ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns

Toppings:

  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, gently mix ground beef with Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Divide mixture into 4 equal portions and form into patties slightly larger than your buns (they'll shrink when cooking).
  3. Make a slight indentation in the center of each patty to prevent bulging during cooking.
  4. Preheat grill or skillet to medium-high heat. Cook patties for 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness.
  5. If adding cheese, place slices on patties during the last minute of cooking and cover to melt.
  6. Toast the buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Assemble burgers with your favorite toppings and serve immediately.

Notes

For juicier burgers, don't overwork the meat and avoid pressing down on the patties while cooking.

Let burgers rest for 5 minutes after cooking for juices to redistribute.
