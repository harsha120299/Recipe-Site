Greek Salad

15 mins Easy 4 servings

A refreshing Mediterranean salad with crisp vegetables, briny olives, and creamy feta cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 English cucumber, chopped
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives
  • 200g feta cheese, cubed
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, bell pepper, and olives.
  2. Add the cubed feta cheese, being careful not to break it up too much.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Let the salad sit for 5-10 minutes before serving to allow flavors to meld.
  6. Serve with crusty bread for a complete meal.

Notes

For authentic Greek flavor, use Greek feta cheese packed in brine.

Add some chopped fresh mint or parsley for extra freshness.
