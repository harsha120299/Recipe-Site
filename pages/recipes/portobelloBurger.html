Portobello Burger

20 mins Easy 4 servings

A delicious vegetarian alternative to the classic burger, using marinated and grilled Portobello mushrooms as the patty.

Ingredients

  • 4 large Portobello mushroom caps
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, or other toppings (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Brush the mushroom caps with the marinade, making sure to coat both sides.
  3. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium heat.
  4. Grill the mushrooms for about 5 minutes on each side, or until tender and juicy.
  5. Toast the buns on the grill during the last minute of cooking.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the grilled mushrooms on the buns and adding your favorite toppings.

Notes

You can add cheese on top of the mushroom caps during the last minute of grilling to melt it over the mushrooms.

This burger is perfect for vegetarians or anyone looking for a meatless option.
