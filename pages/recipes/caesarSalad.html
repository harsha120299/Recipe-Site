Caesar Salad

15 mins Easy 4 servings

A classic Caesar salad made with fresh romaine lettuce, creamy dressing, croutons, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the lettuce with the Caesar dressing until evenly coated.
  3. Add the croutons and Parmesan cheese, tossing again to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan if desired.

Notes

If you prefer a homemade dressing, you can make one by blending mayonnaise, lemon juice, anchovy paste, Dijon mustard, garlic, and Parmesan.

This salad pairs well with grilled chicken or shrimp for added protein.
