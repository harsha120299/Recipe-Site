Breakfast Burrito

15 mins Easy 2 servings

A hearty breakfast burrito filled with scrambled eggs, sausage (or beans), cheese, and salsa for a delicious start to your day.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs, scrambled
  • 1/2 cup cooked sausage or black beans
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, etc.)
  • 2 flour tortillas
  • 2 tbsp salsa

Instructions

  1. Scramble the eggs in a pan until fully cooked.
  2. If using sausage, cook it in a pan until browned and fully cooked.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a pan for a few seconds on each side.
  4. Place the scrambled eggs, cooked sausage or beans, cheese, and salsa onto the center of each tortilla.
  5. Roll up the tortillas, folding in the sides and wrapping tightly.
  6. If desired, toast the burrito in the pan for a crispy finish.

Notes

Customize your burrito by adding extras like avocado, sour cream, or hot sauce!
