Avocado Toast

10 mins Easy 2 servings

A simple yet FlavorFusion breakfast featuring creamy avocado on toasted bread with customizable toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough or whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 eggs (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Microgreens or sprouts (optional)
  • Everything bagel seasoning (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread until golden and crisp.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mash with a fork to desired consistency.
  4. Drizzle toast with olive oil, then spread the mashed avocado evenly on each slice.
  5. For eggs: Fry or poach eggs to your liking and place on top of avocado.
  6. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes, microgreens, or everything bagel seasoning as desired.
  7. Serve immediately while the toast is still warm.

Notes

For extra flavor, rub the toasted bread with a garlic clove before adding avocado.

Other FlavorFusion toppings include cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, smoked salmon, or balsamic glaze.
