Tiramisu

20 mins + chill time Easy 6 servings

A traditional Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and a rich mascarpone mixture.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups espresso, cooled
  • 1 package ladyfingers
  • 1 lb mascarpone cheese, softened
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions

  1. Whisk mascarpone, heavy cream, sugar, egg yolks, and vanilla extract until smooth and thick.
  2. Dip ladyfingers in espresso and layer them in a 9x9 inch pan.
  3. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining mixture.
  4. Cover and chill for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. Before serving, dust with cocoa powder.

Notes

For a stronger coffee flavor, use more espresso. You can also add a splash of rum or coffee liqueur for an adult twist.
