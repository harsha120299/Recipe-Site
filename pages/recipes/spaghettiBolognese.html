Spaghetti Bolognese

45 mins Easy 4 servings

A rich and hearty Italian classic with ground beef, tomatoes, and Italian herbs served over spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
  • Italian herbs (basil, oregano)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spaghetti, cooked

Instructions

  1. Saut√© onion and garlic in a pan until translucent.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in crushed tomatoes and red wine.
  4. Season with Italian herbs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Let simmer for 30 minutes.
  6. Serve sauce over cooked spaghetti.

Notes

For extra depth, simmer longer or add a splash of milk or cream at the end.

Can be frozen for future meals.
