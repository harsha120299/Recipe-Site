Caprese Salad

10 mins Easy 4 servings

A simple and refreshing salad made with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil drizzled with olive oil and balsamic.

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 ball fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Arrange the sliced tomatoes and mozzarella alternately on a serving plate.
  2. Place fresh basil leaves between the slices.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately, or chill for 10 minutes before serving.

Notes

For a more intense flavor, add a sprinkle of sea salt and freshly cracked black pepper right before serving.

Optional: Garnish with a drizzle of pesto for extra flavor!
