Pasta Alfredo

20 mins Easy 4 servings

A creamy and rich pasta dish made with butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fettuccine
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Melt the butter in a pan and add heavy cream. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss the pasta with the sauce until evenly coated.
  5. Serve hot with extra Parmesan if desired.

Notes

You can add grilled chicken or steamed broccoli for variation.

Use freshly grated cheese for best results.
