Buddha Bowl

25 mins Easy 2 servings

A colorful, nourishing bowl filled with grains, roasted veggies, plant-based protein, and creamy dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
  • 1 cup roasted vegetables (e.g. sweet potato, broccoli)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chickpeas or tofu
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • Dressing of choice (e.g. tahini, lemon-garlic)
  • Optional toppings: seeds, herbs, pickled onions

Instructions

  1. Prepare grain base by cooking quinoa or brown rice.
  2. Roast or steam your choice of vegetables until tender.
  3. Cook chickpeas (seasoned) or pan-fry tofu until golden.
  4. Assemble bowl: start with grain, add veggies, protein, and avocado slices.
  5. Top with dressing and any additional toppings you like.
  6. Serve fresh or store refrigerated for later.

Notes

Mix and match ingredients based on season or preference.

Try adding hummus, sprouts, or a boiled egg for variety.
