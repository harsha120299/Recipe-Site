Pasta Puttanesca

30 mins Easy 4 servings

A bold and flavorful pasta made with garlic, anchovies, olives, capers, and chili in a rich tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 anchovy fillets
  • 1/4 cup olives, sliced
  • 2 tbsp capers
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Sauté garlic and anchovies until fragrant and anchovies dissolve.
  3. Add olives, capers, and chili flakes. Stir for 1 minute.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Toss cooked pasta into the sauce and mix well.
  6. Serve hot with fresh parsley or grated Parmesan (optional).

Notes

Anchovies dissolve completely in the oil, giving deep umami flavor — don't skip them!

Use Kalamata olives for extra briny richness.
