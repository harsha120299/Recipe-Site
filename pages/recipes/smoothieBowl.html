Smoothie Bowl

10 mins Easy 2 servings

A healthy and refreshing smoothie bowl made with blended fruits, topped with granola and fresh toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries (strawberries, blueberries, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds (optional)
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • Fresh fruit toppings (banana slices, berries, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine frozen banana, frozen berries, almond milk, and chia seeds. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and top with granola and fresh fruit.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy your nutritious smoothie bowl!

Notes

You can customize the toppings with nuts, seeds, or coconut flakes. Feel free to experiment with different fruits as well!
