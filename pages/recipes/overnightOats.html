Overnight Oats

5 mins + Overnight Easy 2 servings

A quick and healthy breakfast option, overnight oats are prepared the night before and ready to eat in the morning.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • Fresh fruits for topping (berries, banana, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a jar or bowl, combine the oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, and honey/maple syrup.
  2. Stir until well mixed and cover with a lid or plastic wrap.
  3. Refrigerate overnight (or for at least 4 hours) to let the oats soften and absorb the liquid.
  4. In the morning, stir the oats and top with fresh fruits before serving.

Notes

Feel free to add your favorite toppings like nuts, seeds, or a drizzle of peanut butter for extra flavor!
