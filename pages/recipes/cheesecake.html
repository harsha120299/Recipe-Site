Classic Cheesecake

1 hr 10 mins + chill time Medium 8 servings

A rich and creamy dessert with a graham cracker crust and a smooth cream cheese filling—perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 tbsp sugar (for crust)
  • 4 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and 2 tbsp sugar. Press into the bottom of a springform pan.
  3. Bake crust for 8-10 minutes. Let cool.
  4. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and vanilla. Mix well.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, mixing just until blended.
  6. Pour filling over crust. Smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until center is almost set.
  8. Turn off oven, crack door, and let cool for 1 hour inside.
  9. Chill in refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.

Notes

Top with fresh berries, chocolate ganache, or fruit compote for extra flavor.

Use a water bath while baking to reduce cracking.
