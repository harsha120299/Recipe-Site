Pasta Carbonara

30 mins Medium 4 servings

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese, grated
  • 50g parmesan, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the pancetta and cook until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated cheeses, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. When the pasta is al dente, reserve a cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  5. Working quickly, add the hot pasta to the skillet with pancetta, then remove from heat.
  6. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring constantly. Add pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Season with salt if needed and serve immediately with extra grated cheese and black pepper.

Notes

Traditional carbonara doesn't use cream. The creaminess comes from the egg and cheese emulsion.
