BBQ Chicken Sandwich

25 mins Easy 4 servings

Shredded chicken coated in savory BBQ sauce, piled onto a toasted bun and topped with crunchy slaw for a delicious sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 4 sandwich buns
  • 1 cup coleslaw (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with BBQ sauce.
  2. Heat the chicken mixture in a skillet over medium heat until warmed through.
  3. Toast the sandwich buns lightly on a grill or in a skillet.
  4. Assemble the sandwiches by placing the BBQ chicken on the bottom half of each bun.
  5. Top with coleslaw, if desired, and place the top bun to finish.

Notes

You can add pickles, cheese, or any other toppings you like to enhance the flavor.
