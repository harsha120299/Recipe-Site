Vegetable Lo Mein

25 mins Easy 3-4 servings

A savory noodle stir-fry packed with colorful vegetables and tossed in a flavorful soy-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz noodles (Lo Mein or spaghetti)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup bell pepper strips
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • Optional: green onions, sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  4. Add carrots, bell peppers, and cabbage. Stir-fry for 3â€“5 minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Add cooked noodles, soy sauce, and sesame oil to the pan.
  6. Toss everything together until well coated and heated through.
  7. Garnish with green onions or sesame seeds if desired. Serve hot.

Notes

Add tofu or edamame for extra protein.

Use tamari for a gluten-free version.
